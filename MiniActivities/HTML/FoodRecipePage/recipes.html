Rotisserie Chicken Ingredients
  • Whole Chicken:This recipe is meant for a whole 3-pound chicken. If your chicken is larger or smaller, you'll have to adjust the cooking time.

  • Butter: Butter keeps the chicken moist and juicy, while giving the seasonings something to stick to.

  • Seasonings: The rotisserie chicken is simply seasoned with salt, pepper, and paprika
How to Rotisserie a Chicken
  1. Initial Process Season the chicken cavity with salt and place it on a rotisserie (tie the wings and legs if you have twine or string available). Set the grill on high and cook for 10 minutes. Turn the grill down to medium and baste with a mixture of the butter and the seasonings. Close the lid and continue cooking, basting occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through. The internal temperature should reach at least 180 degrees F.

  2. After step A cooked rotisserie chicken should stay good for about four days in a shallow, airtight container in the refrigerator.

  3. Cut Rotisserie Chicken
    • Place the chicken, breast-side up, on a cutting board.
    • Pull one of the legs away from the body and slice through the meat until the hip bone is exposed. Then, pull the leg harder until the leg is detached.
    • Use your knife to separate the drumstick and thigh.
    • Pull the wing tip away from the breast until you can easily cut through the joint.
    • Slice one of the breasts away from the breastbone. Repeat with the other breast.

How To Make Honey Chicken Kabobs
  • Step 1: To make the marinade: Whisk honey, soy sauce, oil, and black pepper together in a large glass bowl.

  • Step 2: Remove 1/4 cup of the marinade to a small jar; seal and set aside to use while cooking.

  • Step 3: Add chicken, onions, bell peppers, and garlic to the marinade in the large bowl. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for 2 hours or overnight.

  • Step 4: When ready to cook, preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.

  • Step 5: Drain marinade from chicken and vegetables; discard marinade. Thread chicken and vegetables alternately onto skewers.

  • Step 6: Place kabobs on the preheated grill. Cook, turning frequently and brushing with reserved marinade, until nicely browned on all sides and chicken is no longer pink in the center, about 12 to 15 minutes.

  • Step 7: Serve hot and enjoy!
